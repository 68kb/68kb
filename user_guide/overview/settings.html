Settings

The settings section allows you to change most of the way 68KB works. Inside this section it includes:

  • Main Settings
  • Templates & Themes
  • Modules
  • Stats
  • Utilities

Settings

The settings allow to change your sites title, keywords, description, email, comments, and the max search results.

These are the only ones that can be changed from the administration. Others can be changed in the includes/config.php file.
